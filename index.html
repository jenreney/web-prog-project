World's NO.1 Pet Fair "All Pets Unite"

This is a fair made in order for people to know of what kind of pets that can be kept, especially the ones that is unique and not the every day, typical type of pets. In this fair, you can enjoy variety of events going on at the same time, such as "Showcase your Pet", a talk about what it is like to have a pet that is not norm, an area for you to talk with other pet owner and play with their pet, and many more!

Type of Events

Showcase your pet!

Have been taking good care of your pet? Want to let the world know about the tricks your pet can do? Here is the place you can showcase your pet all you want to everyone! The winner gets to bring back home a prize worth RM1,500, so do join!


Consult with the Pros

In this fair, we have invited several veterinarians, animal researchers, and famous pet owners to come over and give a talk about pets! There will also be a consultant session with them so they can help you with any pet problem you may have.


Make more pet friends!

We have designated a big area where you can let your pet roam free and make friends with other pets! Not only your pet gets to make new friends, we also prepared tables and chairs for you to sit and relax and also, talk and make friends with other pet owners!

The cheapest Pet Shop!

We will also provide a shop for you to buy your pet purchases. All of our goods are the well-branded type that will surely suits your pet's taste. But don't worry, the goods here are always cheaper than what you can buy from the pet stores!

Who are we? "Pet Diversity"

We are a group of people that wants to bring awareness of pets and joys regarding pets to those who owns and also do not owns one as well. We hold our fair every three per year, one at the beginning of the year, another at the middle of the year and the last at the end of the year.
